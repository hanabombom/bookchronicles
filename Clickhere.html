Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone
On his eleventh birthday, Harry discovers he is a wizard and is whisked away to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He makes new friends, uncovers magical secrets, and learns of the dark wizard Voldemort, who killed his parents. Together with his friends, Harry faces challenges that test his courage and sense of right and wrong
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
In his second year at Hogwarts, Harry encounters strange attacks on students and a mysterious voice only he can hear. He discovers the existence of the legendary Chamber of Secrets and a deadly monster hidden within. With the help of his friends, Harry must uncover the truth and save the school from danger.
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
Harry learns that Sirius Black, a dangerous prisoner who betrayed his parents, has escaped from Azkaban. Throughout the year, he uncovers surprising truths about Black, the events of his parents’ past, and the secrets surrounding his own life. Dark creatures called Dementors add to the tension, testing Harry’s courage and skill.
Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
Harry is unexpectedly entered into the dangerous Triwizard Tournament, facing magical tasks that challenge him physically and mentally. As the competition unfolds, dark forces begin to rise, and Harry confronts Voldemort’s growing power. Friendship, bravery, and trust are tested as Hogwarts faces threats both inside and outside its walls.
Harry Potter and the Order of Phoenix
With Voldemort’s return, Harry struggles to convince the wizarding world of the looming threat. Hogwarts is under strict control, and Harry forms a secret group to teach his friends defense spells against dark magic. He experiences loss, frustration, and the growing burden of responsibility while preparing for the battles to come.
Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince
As Voldemort gains strength, Harry learns about the dark wizard’s past and discovers a mysterious book that reveals new magical knowledge. Friendships are tested, and loyalties questioned as danger grows closer. The year is marked by tragedy, love, and revelations that shape Harry’s path toward the final confrontation.
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows
Harry, Ron, and Hermione embark on a perilous quest to destroy Voldemort’s Horcruxes and defeat him once and for all. The journey is filled with sacrifice, secrets, and revelations about the wizarding world. Their courage and friendship are tested to the limit as they confront Voldemort in a battle that will decide the fate of magic forever.
Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them
Magizoologist Newt Scamander arrives in 1920s New York with a suitcase full of magical creatures, intending only to observe and protect them. However, some creatures escape, creating chaos in the city and drawing the attention of the Magical Congress of the United States. Newt’s adventures reveal dark forces at play and the growing tension between magical and non-magical worlds.
Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Gellert Grindelwald
The dark wizard Grindelwald has escaped custody and is gathering followers to dominate both the magical and non-magical worlds. Albus Dumbledore enlists Newt Scamander to help thwart Grindelwald’s plans, leading them across Europe in search of clues. Secrets are revealed, loyalties tested, and the boundaries between good and evil blur as the battle for the future of magic escalates.
Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore
Dumbledore must confront Grindelwald once again, enlisting Newt and his friends to stop the dark wizard’s rise. Along the way, they uncover hidden truths about family, loyalty, and power, leading to shocking revelations. The final battle between light and dark tests courage, strategy, and sacrifice, shaping the fate of the wizarding world.
